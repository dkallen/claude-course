Module 4 Quick Reference

Goals

The desired state. Organize them hierarchically.

Problems

Current obstacles blocking specific goals. Look for root causes.

Solutions

Actions that resolve problems or directly advance goals.

Risks

Forecasted problems caused by chosen solutions.

Scope

The boundary deciding what is relevant to this effort.

Alignment Check

  • Does this solve a real problem?
  • Does it directly advance a goal?
  • If not, why is it in the plan?
