Module 4: Scenario Quiz

1. A team says “build analytics dashboards” but cannot name the goal. What is missing?

The desired state. The feature may still be useful, but without a named goal you cannot judge alignment or scope.

2. A proposed solution does not resolve a problem or advance a goal. What should happen?

Run the alignment check. Either reveal the hidden goal/problem behind it or discard/defer it.

3. Why is scope not optional?

Because many good ideas are still out of bounds for the current initiative. Scope is what turns thinking into a real brief.

4. What is the difference between a problem and a risk?

A problem exists now; a risk is a forecasted future problem caused by a chosen solution.

5. Why distinguish root cause problems from effect problems?

Because solving symptoms can create motion without progress.

6. What is Claude best used for in this module?

Surfacing assumptions, testing alignment, and helping turn informal thinking into an explicit brief.
